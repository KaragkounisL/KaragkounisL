## 🔬 Research Interests
Adaptive Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) and Reinforcement Learning (RL) for cybersecurity defense.
* **Explainable AI**: Trust calibration and human-centric interpretability in decision-support systems.
* **Reinforcement Learning**: Optimization of pedagogical policies and defensive response mechanisms.
* **Intelligent Tutoring Systems**: Personalized instruction and skill acquisition in adversarial contexts.
* **Neuro-symbolic AI**: Integrating statistical learning with symbolic reasoning and logical constraints.
